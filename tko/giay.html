Biomimicry Technology

Our innovative technologies are inspired by nature's most efficient designs, bringing the outdoors to your performance.

TreeGrip™ Outsole

Inspired by how tree roots anchor to the ground, our TreeGrip™ outsole technology provides exceptional traction on any surface. The multi-directional root-like patterns adapt to terrain changes, giving you confidence with every step.

  • 50% better traction on wet surfaces
  • Self-cleaning channels prevent mud buildup
  • Adaptive grip pattern adjusts to surface changes

LeafFlex™ Upper

Our revolutionary LeafFlex™ upper technology mimics the structure of leaves, creating a material that's simultaneously flexible, breathable, and supportive. The vein-like fiber arrangement adapts to your foot's natural movement.

  • Breathable micro-vents inspired by leaf stomata
  • Adaptive support that moves with your foot
  • 30% lighter than conventional materials

Sustainable by Nature

Our commitment to the environment goes beyond inspiration—we're creating products that respect and protect the natural world.

Recycled Materials

Each pair of shoes contains the equivalent of 12 recycled plastic bottles, diverting waste from landfills and oceans.

One Tree Planted

For every pair sold, we plant a tree in deforested areas, helping to restore natural habitats and combat climate change.

Carbon Neutral

We offset 100% of our carbon emissions from production and shipping, making your footprint lighter on the planet.
